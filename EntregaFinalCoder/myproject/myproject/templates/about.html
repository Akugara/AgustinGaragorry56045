Why I Created This Website

For my final project at Coderhouse, I have envisioned a unique platform that allows collaborative storytelling. This concept is inspired by a classic Uruguayan game, where children weave stories together using small pieces of paper during school hours. The core idea is to create a space where people can write and express themselves in a joint narrative, seamlessly integrating their ideas with those of others.
Imagine the excitement of adapting your story to intertwine with someone else's and the thrill of influencing the direction of a shared narrative. This project aims to recapture the joy of collaborative storytelling, albeit in a modern digital format. Due to time constraints, I had to simplify the project's complexity, but the essence of playful, collective creativity remains intact. Let's see what fascinating stories emerge from this experience!
